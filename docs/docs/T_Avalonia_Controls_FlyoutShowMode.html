Standard0Behavior is typical of a flyout shown reactively, like a context menu. The open flyout takes focus. For a CommandBarFlyout, it opens in it's expanded state.
Transient1Behavior is typical of a flyout shown proactively. The open flyout does not take focus.
TransientWithDismissOnPointerMoveAway2The flyout exhibits Transient behavior while the cursor is close to it, but is dismissed when the cursor moves away.
